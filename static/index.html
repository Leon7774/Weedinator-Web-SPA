WEEDINATOR 9000

Detects weeds in one click!

The system is trained to classify and detect 22 specific classes of flora, including Oryza sativa (Rice) and various invasive weed species such as Alternanthera philoxeroide and Echinochloa crus-galli. This web-based inference engine serves as an easy no-setup way to detect and classify weed in your fields.
