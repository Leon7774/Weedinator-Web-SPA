WEEDINATOR 9000

Detects weeds in one click!

Galileon A. Destura

Theo Benedict S. Pasia

The system is trained to classify and detect 22 specific classes of flora, including Oryza sativa (Rice) and various invasive weed species such as Alternanthera philoxeroide and Echinochloa crus-galli. This web-based inference engine serves as the preliminary validation stage for a standalone hardware device intended for automatic weed targeting.
